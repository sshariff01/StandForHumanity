Featured Cause

#StandForPalestine

Israel's continued military occupation and confiscation of privately owned land in the West Bank, and control over Gaza, are extremely oppressive, with Palestinians having minimal control over their lives. Over 10,000 Palestinian men, women, and children are held in Israeli prisons. Few of them have had a legitimate trial; physical abuse and torture are frequent. Palestinian borders (even internal ones) are controlled by Israeli forces. Periodically men, women, and children are strip searched; people are beaten; women in labor are prevented from reaching hospitals (at times resulting in death); food and medicine are blocked from entering Gaza, producing an escalating humanitarian crisis. Israeli forces invade almost daily, injuring, kidnapping, and sometimes killing inhabitants.

One cannot describe the situation in Gaza in any other term except 'genocide’. Israel has been pounding the Gaza Strip for almost four weeks, which killed at least 1,458 Palestinians and injured more than 8,300 others, an alarming portion of which are innocent, women, children, and youth, according to the Palestinian health ministry.

Leadership in Canada, the United States, and U.K. have shown an astounding level of support for the state of Israel throughout the relentless violence and disproportionate aggression against the citizens of Palestine in an effort to create a Jewish-only state as part of the Zionist movement. Largely due to special-interest lobbying, U.S. taxpayers give Israel an average of $8 million per day, and since its creation have given more U.S. funds to Israel than to any other nation. As citizens around the world are learning about how Israel is using tax dollars, donations, and profits from trade, many are calling for an end to these expenditures.

Support the cause to put an end to the world’s support for Israel and the destruction of Palestinian lives, families, and homes. Take a stand for humanity and put an end to the desecration of innocent lives. Join the movement.

100% of funds raised for this cause are donated directly to Gaza through Islamic Relief

Other Ways You Can Help

1. Boycott Israel - More on the Boycott

People of good conscience have chosen to boycott israeli products and companies supporting the zionist entity.

2. Support the work of other NGOs active on the question of Palestine

To find a list of some other organisations working to improving the situation of Palestinian civilians please click here.

3. Write to your elected (or unelected) representatives asking them to take action

Send a letter to your politicians to express your concern for the situation in Palestine, particularly in Gaza where citizens are currently under attack. As more people write to their governments these governments will understand that the question of Palestine is no longer one they can ignore – at home or abroad. Let your voice be heard.

4. Take to the streets, or the theatres, or anywhere you can

More action is required to let people in authority know the strength of feeling about this question and a very visible way of doing this is by taking to the streets in protest at the conspiracy of silence practised by governments on the question of Palestine. However street events don’t always have to be sombre or serious events – use your imagination and organise carnivals and festivals which educate your community about Palestinian food, dress, music, poetry and other aspects of the culture. Such events can be an opportunity to explain to people that this culture is being suffocated and eradicated by the belligerent Israeli occupation.

What is Stand For Humanity?

Stand for Humanity is a social movement dedicated to supporting the lives of innocent people in times of need. For each respective cause, each person on the map represents an important piece of the puzzle to taking a stand for humanity by either donating to the cause or raising awareness to it. All funds raised by Stand for Humanity are donated directly to charity funds supporting the featured cause.

100% of funds raised for #StandForPalestine go directly to Islamic Relief

Islamic Relief has been responding to emergencies for three decades, providing a lifeline for vulnerable communities affected by disaster and poverty. We continue to increase the work we do to protect those living in high-risk areas, with a focus on innovative disaster-risk reduction and climate-change adaptation.
